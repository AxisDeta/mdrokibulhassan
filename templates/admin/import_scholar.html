How to Import

Enter your Google Scholar profile URL to automatically import all your publications.

  • Go to your Google Scholar profile
  • Copy the URL from your browser (e.g., https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=YOUR_ID)
  • Paste it below and click "Import Publications"
  • The system will extract all publications with titles, authors, venues, years, and citations
  • Duplicate publications will be automatically skipped
Example: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=jic1XEcAAAAJ&hl=en
